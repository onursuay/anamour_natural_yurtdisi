Our Story

About Anamour

Women's craftsmanship meets the purest form of nature — creating flavors that inspire tables around the world.

We set out with the dream of producing inspiring flavors for tables by bringing together the purest form of nature with women's craftsmanship. Fruits prepared with the right methods, of the highest quality, carefully selected, and processed in a way that preserves their nutritional value… All within a production model where women are at the heart of the process.

Over the years, we have built a strong team of experts in fruit drying, most of whom are women, passionate about their work. They are masters of both traditional craftsmanship and modern technology, carefully selecting and delicately drying each fruit.

We dry fruits sourced from our fertile lands in hygienic, closed systems while preserving their nutritional value, delivering their flavor to tables all year round. Today, we continue with the same entrepreneurial spirit, proudly delivering our products to all corners of the world while supporting women's labor and local agriculture.
